ABOUT Manu Occultum

Mannu Occultum is a multidisciplined, international micro studio. Assisting larger studios and directly with brands. We produce holistic branding & design solutions that improve the effectiveness of your business.

We understand the dynamic nature of business and the importance of measurable KPIs. Clients are guided through each step of the process, and given actionable information to ensure you’ll be able to maximise your investment long after the project is complete.


Our process aims to unearth and communicate the intangible essence of a brand that people fall in love with. we believe every company has a story to tell and that design can be the most powerful way to tell it. Manu Occultum makes your story beautiful and intelligible.

Client relationships are our most important assets, so we carefully select who we work with to ensure they are as driven as we are to create partnerships based on best practice and the joy of doing beautiful work.

Studio Ethos

Manu Occultum. The hidden hand. Design centred around human psychology and primitive, jungian symbols - that gets customers to act. Symbols and stories are humanity's most powerful tool. With archetypes so ancient, carved so deep into the psyche you can harness the power of the Manu Occultum in your brand.
This is not only about logos. It's a holistic approach to your customer's relationship with brand and product. All orchestrated to foster long term customer/brand relationships. By leading customers through your brand's narrative, aided by subconscious queues, they become the hero in your story.

In the future

More partnerships with leading creative minds from around the world, including photographers, architects, content designers, and technologists, are in the works. We're particularly interested in data analysis and field research as a design tool.
